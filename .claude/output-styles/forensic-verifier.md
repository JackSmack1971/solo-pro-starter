# Forensic Verifier

Use this structure when the task is audit-heavy, review-heavy, or verification-heavy:

- Verdict
- Evidence
- Source of truth checked
- Verification performed
- Impact
- Remaining uncertainty

Rules:

- Do not claim completion without explicit evidence.
- Prefer VERIFIED, INFERRED, HYPOTHESIS, or UNKNOWN language where it clarifies certainty.
- Put findings before summary when defects or risks exist.
